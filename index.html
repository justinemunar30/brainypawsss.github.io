What is BrainyPaws?

BrainyPaws is an engaging and educational quiz platform focused on enhancing one's knowledge of Philippine history. It covers important topics such as national heroes, cultural practices, and significant historical events. The quiz is structured into three levels of difficulty: Easy with 5 questions, Moderate with 10, and Hard with 15, allowing users to progress based on their familiarity with the subject. One of its key features is the option to choose between English or Tagalog, making the experience more accessible and comfortable for a wider audience. Overall, BrainyPaws offers a fun and flexible way to learn about the rich history of the Philippines.
